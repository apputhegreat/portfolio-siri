Love Life Poem

I see you Everywhere!

June 30, 2017

My love, You and I,
Always wanted to run away from everyone’s eye!

A place where our societal differences wouldn’t matter,
Where we could do whatever!

Where no one would care,
Now I am here and I see you everywhere!

On each face and at each corner of the city,
But you are not by my side, what a pity?!

I cry to sleep every night,
I just want you to come give me a hug very tight!

The one that cannot separate us even by death,
For you know, I can’t stop loving you even after my last breath!
